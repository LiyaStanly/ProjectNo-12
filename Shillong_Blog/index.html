Shillong: A Serene Escape

Welcome to the "Scotland of the East" - a city of breathtaking beauty, cascading waterfalls, and vibrant culture. Explore the tranquility of Shillong, where every corner tells a story.

Why Shillong?

Shillong, the capital of Meghalaya, is renowned for its lush green landscapes, clean air, and pristine natural beauty. It's a city that effortlessly blends modern life with traditional Khasi culture. From the gentle misty hills to the lively local markets, Shillong offers a unique experience that captivates every visitor.

What sets Shillong apart is its commitment to cleanliness and a deep-rooted respect for nature. The city's clean streets and well-maintained public spaces are a testament to the local community's pride in their home. It's a place where you can truly reconnect with nature and find peace away from the urban hustle.

Featured Highlights

The Tranquil Umiam Lake

Often called Barapani, this vast lake is a perfect spot for boating and enjoying stunning sunsets. Its calm waters and green surroundings make it a photographer's paradise.

Majestic Elephant Falls

Named after an elephant-shaped rock, this three-tiered waterfall is a sight to behold. The lush green surroundings and the roar of the water create an unforgettable experience.

A Walk Through Police Bazaar

The heart of Shillong's commerce and culture. Police Bazaar is a bustling market where you can find everything from traditional handicrafts to delicious local street food.
