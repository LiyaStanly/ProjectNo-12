Top Attractions in Shillong

Discover the natural wonders and charming spots that make Shillong a must-visit destination. From serene lakes to majestic waterfalls, each place offers a unique experience.

Umiam Lake (Barapani)

Located about 15 km north of Shillong, this stunning reservoir is a popular spot for water sports and picnics. Surrounded by lush hills and pine trees, it’s a perfect place for a peaceful getaway and boating.

Elephant Falls

A beautiful three-tiered waterfall located just outside the city. Named by the British due to a rock resembling an elephant (which was destroyed in an earthquake), the falls are a major draw. The well-maintained path and surrounding flora make it a pleasant trek.

Shillong Peak

The highest point in Shillong, offering a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire city and the Himalayas on a clear day. It's an ideal spot for photography and enjoying a cool, misty climate.

Ward’s Lake

Also known as Nan Polok, this horseshoe-shaped artificial lake is a popular recreational spot in the heart of the city. Visitors can enjoy boating, feeding the fish from the wooden bridge, or simply strolling through the manicured botanical gardens.

Police Bazaar (PB)

The commercial hub of Shillong, Police Bazaar is a vibrant market where you can experience the local life. It's filled with shops, restaurants, and stalls selling everything from traditional handicrafts to modern apparel.
